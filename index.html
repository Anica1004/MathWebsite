My Story

Throughout my academic journey, I have dedicated myself to learning and exploring various mathematical concepts. From high school mathematics to university-level courses, I have always had a deep passion for mathematics.

My enthusiasm for math extends beyond my own studies - tutoring math has been a true passion of mine. I find immense joy in teaching and helping others understand complex mathematical concepts, which has further fueled my desire to contribute to math education initiatives.

I understand that some students may find math challenging, and I am committed to supporting them every step of the way. Whether it's providing additional explanations, offering personalized tutoring sessions, or simply being a supportive mentor, I am dedicated to helping students overcome their struggles and succeed in math.

I believe that everyone should have access to quality math education, regardless of their background or circumstances. This belief is what drives my commitment to supporting this non-profit initiative with my knowledge and skills.

With a strong foundation in mathematics and a background in computer science, I am excited to collaborate with like-minded individuals to develop and curate resources that make math education more accessible and engaging for all. Together, we can empower learners and inspire a love for math that extends beyond the classroom.

I am dedicated to making a meaningful impact through this initiative and look forward to contributing to the success of students. This website marks my first step in contributing to the educational community, and I am eager to embark on this journey of making a positive impact in the field of math education.
